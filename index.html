Heritage

Born in Parma.
Worn in bed.

Sonno di Maiale™ is produced in a small-batch facility in Langhirano, the village that has been air-curing ham in mountain breezes since the Roman Empire. Our master slicer, Enzo, uses a hand-operated berkel to achieve the sub-millimeter translucency required for optimal light occlusion.

Each mask is sliced to order, individually laid on food-grade silk paper, and shipped in a refrigerated artisan box lined with rosemary sprigs because Enzo insists on it and we are not going to argue with Enzo.
